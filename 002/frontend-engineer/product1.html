About Kosovo Football Federation

The Football Federation of Kosovo (KFF) is the official governing body for association football in Kosovo. Established to promote and develop football across the nation, the KFF oversees all national teams and football development programs.

Kosovo's national football team competes in international competitions including World Cup qualifiers, UEFA Nations League, and friendlies. With recent improvements in performance and infrastructure, Kosovo is emerging as a competitive force in European football.
